### About Game
* Title : Turtle Runaway
* You are the red chaser Turtle, Catch the blue runner Turtle within the given time
* Before you start, you can see rules top-left
* After you start, you can see left time and whether you catch or not
* After the game end, you can see your score

### Score And Rules
* You have 15 seconds
* You can get 100 points if you catch in 2 seconds
* If you don't catch in 2 seconds, the score will be minus sequentially
* If runner turtle go out of the side, you will get 0 points
* Also if you don't catch in given time, you will get 0 points

### How To Play Game
* You can start by pressing "Enter" key
* "Left" key  : Turn left 10 degrees
* "Right" key : Turn right 10 degrees
* "Up" key    : Step forward 10 pixels
* "Down" key  : Step backward 10 pixels
